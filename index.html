Our Story in Pictures

Where

Finca La Marabulla

Ronda, Málaga, Andalucía

Perched in the hills of the Sierra de las Nieves, Finca La Marabulla is a beautifully restored old Spanish farmhouse surrounded by olive groves and the sweeping Andalusian countryside. It will be our home for the wedding ceremony and dinner.

Ronda itself is one of Spain's most dramatic towns with its famous white houses and bridge spanning el Tajo, with views that have inspired writers and travellers for centuries. We can't wait to share it with you.

Getting Here

Travel & Stay

✈️

By Air

The closest international airports are in Málaga and Sevilla. There are direct flights to both from Dublin, Cork and across Europe. If you are coming from outside Europe, Málaga is your closest option for transatlantic connections. You can also fly to Madrid, Barcelona or Faro (Portugal) if you want to make a longer holiday of it.

🚗

By Car

Ronda is approximately 110 km (1hr 20mins) from Málaga airport and 140km from Sevilla (2hrs) by car. Car hire is available at all nearby airports.

🚆

By Train

Ronda has a train station with direct services from Málaga (2.5 hrs) and connections from Madrid. A scenic and relaxed way to arrive.

🏨

Where to Stay

Ronda has a wide range of accommodation, from boutique hotels to Villas. As Ronda does not have a lot of taxis we advise that you stay in the town itself, we will arrange transportation from the town centre to the Finca on the day of the wedding. We recommend booking early as May is a popular time.
