Where

Finca La Marabulla

Ronda, Málaga, Andalucía

Perched in the hills of the Serranía de Ronda, Finca La Marabulla is a stunning private estate surrounded by olive groves and sweeping Andalusian countryside. It will be our home for the wedding ceremony, dinner, and the next morning's pool party.

Ronda itself is one of Spain's most dramatic towns — a white hilltop city spanning a deep gorge, with views that have inspired travellers for centuries. We can't wait to share it with you.

Getting Here

Travel & Stay

✈️

By Air

The closest international airports are Málaga Costa del Sol (AGP) — about 100km — and Gibraltar (GIB) about 90km. Both have good connections from across Europe.

🚗

By Car

Ronda is well connected by road. From Málaga airport take the A-357 west, then the A-367 south to Ronda — approximately 1.5 hours. Car hire is available at all nearby airports.

🚆

By Train

Ronda has a train station with direct services from Málaga (2.5 hrs) and connections from Madrid (via Algeciras). A scenic and relaxed way to arrive.

🏨

Where to Stay

Ronda has a wide range of accommodation — from boutique hotels in the old town to rural fincas in the surrounding hills. We recommend booking early as May is a popular time. More details to follow.
